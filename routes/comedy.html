Comedy Movies

This is a movie website featuring four different genres: action, romantic, drama, and comedy.

To access the different genres, you can modify the URL from "genres=action" to "genres=comedy" for example. Alternatively, you can click on buttons to navigate through the genres and observe how the URL changes accordingly.

To navigate to the home page, please modify the URL from "localhost:3030/?genres=action" to "localhost:3030," for example. This will take you back to the home page of the movie website.
